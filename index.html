About

I'm Asraful Islam — a tech enthusiast, writer, and perpetual wonderer based on the internet. I spend my days thinking about where technology is taking us and writing essays that try to make sense of it all.

My writing sits at the intersection of emerging technology, digital society, and the quieter human questions that rarely make the headlines. I'm less interested in hype than in what actually changes the way we live and work.

When I'm not writing, I'm reading, building small things, or staring at a blank page wondering if the idea I had was actually good.
